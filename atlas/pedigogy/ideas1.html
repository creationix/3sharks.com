Atlas #1 Project Ideas

Atlas Project Ideas:

Updated: 3/8/99

Atlas #1, Physical Geography and/or Mapping


A. PHYSICAL GEOGRAPHY:


A.1. Soils, underlying structures and their history

1. Is most of Conway built on soil deemed unsuitable for urbanization due to its poor drainage characteristics? What is the drainage situation in Conway?


A.2. Earthquakes

1. Is Conway prepared for the next big one?

2. Why do we have earthquakes in Arkansas? We are in the middle of a tectonic plate.


A.3. Erosion (as it shapes the land forms, constraints to development)


A.4. Rivers and streams (source, location, usages, pollutants, etc.)

1. What are the major sources and/or causes of water pollution in Conway, in the region, in the state?

2. What is the major source of Conway’s drinking water? Is it at risk?

3. How clean is the water that Conway expels downstream? Is Conway, either as a city, or as a developed area (runoff erosion into rivers, etc), a major water polluter?


A.5. Groundwater (source, location, pollutants)

1. What is the status of the state’s aquifers. Are they being polluted, and by what? Are the water levels dropping, and at what rates. Could our aquifers run dry? Where are the recharge areas for our aquifers? Are they in danger?


A.6. Air (temperature, wind speed and direction, pollutants)

1. Can the lapse rate alone account for the differences in temperature across the state?

2. What are the major sources and/or causes of air pollution in Conway, in the state?


A.6.a Tornadoes

1. Why do tornadoes follow I-30? What does topology have to do with it?


A.7. Climate (temperature, moisture, seasons, determinants)

1. Does it rain more on the weekends than during the week in Arkansas. What about surrounding states?


A.8. Ecosystems: Tie the systems together

1. How does the Southern Oscillation effect Arkansas weather?


A.9. Pollution (sources, effects)

1. What hazardous materials are used, stored, or buried on UCA campus? Do we have toxic waste sites on campus? Are they a threat? Where are they? Ditto for Conway.

2. Exactly how much pollution does a car, truck and SUV produce? And what kinds of pollutants?


A.10. Human Impacts: Plants and Animals (exotics, hunting, farming)

1. How do we manage white tailed deer. Are they becoming a problem in urban areas?

2. Is the exotic animal Nutria a problem in Arkansas yet? It burrows into dikes and levies weakening them in the process. Are some communities in danger of flooding because of this animal?

3. How goes our talapia farming? We imported this exotic fish to fish farm. Is it a big industry in the state? Can they live in the wild? Are they taking over?


A.11. Human Impacts: Agriculture (location, crops, markets, inputs, outputs)

1. What impact does rice growing have on the state (use of water, manpower, fertilizers, pesticides, water pollution, etc.). Why do we grow rice (it isn’t native)?


A.12. Human Impacts: Development (type, location, inputs, products, outputs)

1. What happens to all the left over or thrown out food from the cafeteria and the food court. Is it much? Could it somehow be reused (feed animals, compost, etc.)?

2. What is UCA’s waste stream contain? Does UCA recycle aluminum cans, glass bottles, paper, newsprint, etc? Why or why not? How big is UCA’s waste stream.

3. Given the health concerns about Aspartame or Nutrasweet/Equal, what chemical additives are put in cafeteria/food court food. Could you eat chemically free on campus?

4. How soon will Conway need a new landfill? How much will it cost and are there even potential sites left in the county?


C. GEOGRAPHIC INFORMATION SYSTEMS


C.1. Availability of Maps

1. What is the availability of soil and drainage maps of either the UCA campus or Conway over the last 20 years?


C.2. Mapping the Atlas

1. Construct a relief map of Conway or Faulkner County from quad or topo maps.
